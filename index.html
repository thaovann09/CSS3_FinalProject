Cat Tien National Park

Cat Tien National Park, located in southern Vietnam, is a sprawling UNESCO Biosphere Reserve renowned for its rich biodiversity and lowland rainforest ecosystems. Covering over 720 square kilometers, it's a haven for diverse flora and fauna, including rare species of animals and plants. The park offers various activities like trekking, wildlife viewing, and exploring unique wetland areas like Bau Sau (Crocodile Lake).
